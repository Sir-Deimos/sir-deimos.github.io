2025 Event Schedule

Join us for an action-packed 2025 season at West KY Autocross! Our events are held at Beacon Dragway in Paducah, KY, offering a thrilling mix of competition and community. Check out the details below and mark your calendars!

Location: Beacon Dragway, Paducah, KY

Start Time: 7:30 AM CDT

Entry Fees: $50 to race | $5 to spectate | $10 to ride

Kick off the season with our first autocross event of 2025! Gates open at 7:30 AM, with racing starting at 9:00 AM. Whether you're driving, watching, or hitching a ride, this is the perfect way to start the year. Pre-register here>>> Facebook Event

Start Time: 8:00 AM CDT

Entry Fees: $50 to race | $5 to spectate | $10 to ride

Autumn brings cooler temps and hotter competition. Test your skills on a fresh course design as the season winds down. RSVP on the Facebook Event page!
